About MLA

The "Ecole Normale Sup√©rieure (ENS), Meknes" organizes the 3rd International Conference on Machine Learning Applications (MLA 2022).
MLA 2022 will provide an excellent international forum for sharing knowledge and results in theory, methodology and applications of Machine Learning. The Conference aims to disseminate novel ideas, latest technology trends, research developments, and practical applications in the area of machine learning (ML) and its applications. Original and high quality papers related to this theme are solicited, including theories, analysis, applications, and experiments. The MLA 2022 is an opportunity to exchange ideas and build up partnerships and collaborations between experienced researchers and practitioners from several universities, research institutes, and industries.
Authors are invited to submit original unpublished manuscripts that demonstrate recent advances in machine learning.
All accepted papers will be submitted for indexing ( Pending) .
Extended papers (50%) will be published as a chapters in one of specialized Book Series( Pending)

Submit your Paper

Authors are invited to submit original and unpublished work. Manuscripts should respect the conference template, including figures, tables, and references.The paper must be prepared by using the template in MS Word or LaTeX as PDF files through Easychair. The direct link for paper submission is ¬†

Papers are reviewed on the basis that they do not contain plagiarized material and have not been submitted to any other conference at the same time (double submission), Urkund tool will be used to detect plagiarism.
Each paper will be reviewed by at least three regular PC members in tow round of review . The acceptance decisions will take into account paper novelty, technical depth, elegance, practical or theoretical impact, and presentation.
Authors of high quality papers will be invited to submit an extended version of their work for potential publication in a special issue/section of an international journal.
